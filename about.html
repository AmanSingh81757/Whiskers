About Whiskers


Welcome to Whiskers, your go-to resource for finding loving homes for furry friends in need. We are a non-profit organization dedicated to rescuing and rehoming animals from local shelters and fostering environments.
We understand that adopting a pet is a big responsibility, which is why we strive to provide you with all the information and support you need to make the best decision for you and your new companion. Our team of dedicated volunteers and staff work tirelessly to provide care and attention to each and every animal in our program.
We believe that every animal deserves a second chance, and we work hard to match each pet with the perfect forever home. Whether you're looking for a playful puppy, a cuddly kitten, or a mature companion, we have the perfect pet for you.
Thank you for choosing Whiskers for your animal adoption needs. Together, we can give these deserving animals the loving homes they deserve.

Where and How We Operate

Currently we are operational in sector - Faridabad. We take care of stray animals in the nearby localities. If there is any animal who is in need of help we are the first ones to reach at the spot and provide help to the animal.
Generally we receive calls from people who report incidents of animals suffering from diseases or stray animals who are in poor condition. Whenever we come across this situation we first of all give first aid to the animal if needed and immediately take it to the nearest vet.
After getting the whole body checked up we take the animal to our shelter and take care until someone adopts the animal or the animal unfortunately passes away.
