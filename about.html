About Whiskers

Welcome to Whiskers, your go-to resource for finding loving homes for furry friends in need. We are a non-profit organization dedicated to rescuing and rehoming animals from local shelters and fostering environments. We understand that adopting a pet is a big responsibility, which is why we strive to provide you with all the information and support you need to make the best decision for you and your new companion. Our team of dedicated volunteers and staff work tirelessly to provide care and attention to each and every animal in our program. We believe that every animal deserves a second chance, and we work hard to match each pet with the perfect forever home. Whether you're looking for a playful puppy, a cuddly kitten, or a mature companion, we have the perfect pet for you. Thank you for choosing Whiskers for your animal adoption needs. Together, we can give these deserving animals the loving homes they deserve.
