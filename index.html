WHISKERS


Who we are?



Whiskers is a non profitable organisation which aims to provide shleter to homeless animals and give them a new ray of hope. Through this initiative we have given new homes to 100 pets. These include animals like dogs, cats, parrots, squirrel and hamsters. As of today our work is limited to the city of Faridabad but we hope to expand this organisation to different cities very soon. As a fellow animal lover I request you to anopt an animal today and be the reason to put a smile on it's cute face. It's completely free of cost and brings you abundance of joys
