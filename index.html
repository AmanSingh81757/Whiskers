WHISKERS


Who we are?



Whiskers is a non profitable organisation which aims to provide shleter to homeless animals and give them a new ray of hope. Through this initiative we have given new homes to 100 pets. These include animals like dogs, cats, parrots, squirrel and hamsters. As of today our work is limited to the city of Faridabad but we hope to expand this organisation to different cities very soon. As a fellow animal lover I request you to anopt an animal today and be the reason to put a smile on it's cute face. It's completely free of cost and brings you abundance of joys.



OUR AIM

Our main aim is to provide a shelter to stray animals and pets who are abandoned by their owners. According to a report by economics time India has the highest number of homeless stray animals and highest number of pet abandonment in the whole world. This is not only a problem for the poor animals but also for people, as these stray animals are one of the major contributors of accidents in our country. By giving them a proper shelter and proper care we can control their overgrowing population and prevent many roadside accidents. We at Whiskers love animals and we try our very best to give these animals a new home and for some a second chance at life. Starting from Faridabad, we wish to expand the idea and inspire more people to help stray animals and pets and raise awarness about this topic.
